My mother used to garden when I was young, and my brother and I were responsible for picking the weeds. We were the perfect size to crawl in between the plants and pick the tomatoes next to the ground. This childhood memory followed into my adult life and every year you can find me out in my own very own garden planting, picking, and weeding.



Pong was the first video game system that I was introduced back in the 1980s. Whether it was the Atari, the NES, the Genesis, or any of the gaming consoles that came after, I've had them and I've played them. My video game collection spans multiple decades, and it would take a trailer to haul. I don't play many video games anymore, but every one in a while I add to my collection.



A proud single father of three boys, we're active in scouting, hockey, backyard camping, and lots of other stuff. You might find us cheering on the Pointers at a game, on a walk cleaning up litter in the neighborhood, or taking turns on the original Super Mario Bros.
